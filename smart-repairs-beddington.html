SMART repair stands for Small to Medium Area Repair Technique. It covers localised paint damage and minor dents in a defined area without the cost or time of a full panel removal and respray. It is the repair method suited to a single scuff on a door, a shallow scratch on a rear quarter, a cluster of stone chips on a bonnet. At KITH Bodyworks in Hackbridge, SMART repairs are carried out in our controlled workshop environment.

The repair process starts with a thorough clean of the affected area. All wax, polish and contamination removed. The damage is then assessed properly before any preparation begins. A shallow scuff that has not gone through the lacquer may need minimal preparation before colour is applied. A scratch through to the colour coat needs feathering, priming and sealing first. The preparation stage determines the quality of what follows.

FAQs

Common questions

Is a SMART repair as good as a full respray?

For localised damage within the SMART area and carried out to the correct standard, the result should not be distinguishable. For larger areas, a full respray produces a better outcome.

How long does a SMART repair take?

Most are completed the same day, typically within two to four hours.

Can SMART repairs be carried out outside?

No. All SMART repairs are carried out in our controlled workshop where temperature, humidity and dust levels are consistent.
