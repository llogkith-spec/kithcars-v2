How we price variable jobs

Jobs like clutch replacement, timing chains, brake discs and engine work vary in cost because every vehicle is different — a Ford Fiesta clutch uses different parts and takes different labour time to a BMW 5 Series.

We never give vague estimates. When you contact us with your reg plate, we look up your specific vehicle, price the exact parts needed and give you a written quote before any work starts. No surprises — ever.

50% deposit required on jobs £200 and above. Balance due on collection. Jobs under £200 are paid in full on collection.
