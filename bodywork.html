What we do

Complete bodywork and cosmetic restoration.

Whether it's a small scuff or significant accident damage, we approach every job with the same attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Scratch & Scuff Repair

Fast, professional repairs for surface scratches, scuffs and paint chips. We colour-match precisely and blend seamlessly into the surrounding panels.

Dent Removal

Painless dent removal and traditional dent repair techniques to restore your vehicle's shape without the need for filler or a full respray where possible.

Panel & Paint Restoration

Full panel preparation, priming, colour matching and lacquering for a seamless, showroom-quality finish that matches your vehicle's original paint.

Accident Repair

Structural repairs, alignment checks and full cosmetic restoration for accident-damaged vehicles. We liaise with insurance companies and provide detailed reports.

Full & Partial Resprays

Whether you need a single panel resprayed or a full colour change, we deliver lasting, professional results using quality automotive paint systems.

Protective Treatments

Paint protection film, ceramic coatings and other treatments to keep your vehicle looking its best and protect your investment for the long term.
